Guts is the main protagonist of Berserk. Also known as the Black Swordsman, Guts is a former mercenary and member of the Band of Falcão, currently a traveler with the mark of the sacrifice in which He travels the world in constant conflict between achieving his goals and looking after those close to him.

PERSONALITY

Guts has what could be the most complex personality in the entire series. It can be said that due to this complex personality, he is perhaps the most "human" of the characters introduced, demonstrating changes significant changes to the character as he ages.

Having been born into a bloody situation and forced into a life of violence, Guts barely had time to live like a normal child. Although he experienced moments of happiness and joy, his true passion was warped into a desire for fighting, mainly to gain Gambino's acceptance and later to enhance your own combat skills. After successfully killing the first person in what would become a list of thousands, Guts trained himself to become stronger and know how to hold his sword, eventually creating a strong and very personal relationship with the blade he wields.

During his time with the Band of the Hawk, Guts had also told Casca that he was only happy when wielding his sword.
